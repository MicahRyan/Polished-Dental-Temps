REFUND POLICY

Returns without a Receipt: Refunds over $5 will be issued in the form of a merchandise credit or merchandise exchange for the current selling price.

Check Policy: We will accept your personal check imprinted with your name. Checks must be written for the amount of the purchase only.  Checks returned unpaid by your financial institution are subject to a service charge of $25 or the maximum allowed by law.

Refund: The original credit card used for the purchase must be presented in order to process the refund to that card.

Adjustment Policy with an Original Sales Receipt for a Permanent Hire

Polished Dental Temps will attempt to replace a candidate at no additional fee if employment terminates within 30 days from the date of purchase.  If we are unable to find a suitable replacement within 45 days from the departure of the employment we may issue a partial refund in the original form of payment.  The refund will be prorated according to the length of time that the referred candidate was employed.  No refund or credit will be issued for returns with an original sales receipt more than 30 days after the purchase or agreement date.

We are available Monday to Friday, 9:00AM - 6:00PM CST, closed Saturday, Sunday, and holidays.
